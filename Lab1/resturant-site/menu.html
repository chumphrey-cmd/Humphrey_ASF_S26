Our Menu Vision

Welcome to our steam-powered cafe and bakery, where we believe that a beautiful plate is better than an ugly one. Whether you are an airship captain or a mechanical engineer, our meals are designed to elegantly fuel your day. In a bustling world of gears and cogs, our culinary philosophy is straightforward: simple, handcrafted flavors are better than complex, over-engineered dishes.

We Value The Quality of Our Ingredients

When it comes to what goes into our food, explicit is better than implicit. We proudly and openly source our heritage grains from local wind-powered mills and our produce from hydroponic greenhouse terraces. Cultivating top-tier, sustainable agriculture can be a complex process, but we firmly believe that a thoughtful, complex ecosystem is better than a complicated, artificial supply chain.
